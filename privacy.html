Privacy Policy

Facebook Connect

You are connecting to ABFloods using Facebook Connect, and as part of that process you are installing a Facebook Application on your Facebook account. As part of this process we follow the Facebook Platform Policies which you can find at this link: Facebook Platform Policies.

When you connect via Facebook we store your name, profile picture, and Facebook ID. No further information is needed for this application.

Each time you access ABFloods after connecting via Facebook Connect we set a session for that visit so that you can remain logged in. Your session is terminated when you click on the 'Logout' button on the site, and the session is automatically ended after 24 hours of inactivity.

Data Storage

The only Facebook information stored is your name, Facebook ID, and a link to your profile picture. Through the ABFloods service, you are required to provide your address and a request, which is also stored. All of this information is stored with Firebase.
